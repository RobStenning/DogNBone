Welcome to the Dog House

Situated in the heart of Hove (Actually), tucked away in a garden is an 7' x 5' dog house which is a little different on the inside. No funky smells*, no stale water bowl or muddy paw prints just fresh beer poring from 6 keg lines, a 7 gallon fermenter sitting along side a kettle and mash tun.


With enough capacity to brew 19L (5 Gal) at a time you can be sure to find at least 4 different kegs on tap at any time. Usually with something bubbling away in the fermenter, waiting it's turn to be kegged and drunk by friends, family and free-loaders alike.


*we can't gaurantee the smells.

Our Story

From humble begginings to big boy beers.


The one with the big ears is Gimli, on the left if that helps. He's Chief of Biscuit Operatios, meaning he's in charge of flavour profiles, mouth feel, and quality control measures. With years of snout experience behind him, testing some of the finest treats on offer from our local beer outlets you can rest asure your treats are in safe hands.


Right, with the slightly smaller ears is Rob, that's me. I do everything else, from this website to drinking the produce, I mean testing, you know quality control and all that. I've always enjoyed cask beer and as the beer scene has grown I got into my kegged beers, trying all the styles I have a particualr thing for a hoppy, fruity IPA and a sweet spot for a sour. I wanted to learn new skills and home brewing seemed to be the perfect platform to bring them all together. From building a brand to coding and of course brewing beer. Follow me and G on our beery journey, who nows where we will end up, who knows when or where you'll bump into us...
