Welcome to the Dog House

Situated in the heart of Hove, tucked away in a garden is an 7' x 5' dog house which is a little different on the inside. No funky smells*, no stale water bowl, no muddy paw prints just fresh beer poring from 4 keg lines, a 14 gallon fermenter sitting along side a kettle and mash tun.


The Dog House has enough capacity to brew 38L (10 Gal) at a time you can be sure to find at least 4 different kegs on tap at any time. Usually with something bubbling away in the fermenter, waiting its turn to be kegged and drunk by friends, family and free-loaders alike.


*we can't gaurantee the smells.

Pouring Now

Our go to Pale Ale, refreshing, easy drinking with enough hops to keep it interesting. A crowd pleaser just like the trick.

Mango Sour | 3.8% | Sour

Fermented on Mangos and Peach, kettled soured for 48 hours, its our easy going summer sour.

Stanky Dog | 7.2% | New England IPA

Proper stinky, dry hopped to the max with our favourite US hops, more fruit punch than beer just rember it's over 7% yeah...

Sun Dog Millionare | 5.2% | Mexican Lager

Brewed with 5 different malts, topped up with Maple Syrup and Demerera, double fermented, filtered through coffee beans and aged with Bourbon soaked wood chips...

Our Story

From humble begginings to big dog beers.


The one with the big ears is Gimli, on the right if that helps. Chief of Biscuit Operations, he's in charge of flavour profiles, mouth feel, and quality control. With years of experience behind him, testing some of the finest treats on offer from local beer outlets you can rest asure your treats are in safe hands.


Left, with the slightly smaller ears is Rob, that's me. I do everything else, from this website to drinking the produce, I mean testing, you know quality control and all that. I've always enjoyed cask beer and as the beer scene has grown so did my love for kegged beers. I have a particualr thing for a hoppy, fruity IPA's, a sweet spot for a sour and a devotion for the dark stuff. I wanted to learn new skills and home brewing seemed to be the perfect platform to bring them all together, building a brand, coding and of course brewing beer. Follow me and G on our journey, who nows where we will end up, the only gaurantee is beer.
