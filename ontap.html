~ On Tap ~

- PAW -

  • ABV - 4.5%
  • STYLE - Pale Ale
  • IBU - 45
  • DRY HOPS - 6 g/l

Our go to Pale Ale, refreshing and easy drinking like a British Pale but with enough hops to confuse it with an American. We are urrently on v3 of this recipie and this batch was fermented with Verdant's IPA yeast so it is a little more hazy.

- Sun Dog Millionare -

  • ABV - 5.2%
  • STYLE - Mexican Lager
  • IBU - 12
  • DRY HOPS - n/a

Our first lager, based upon the infamous Corona, bittered with a unique variety of hops from New Zealand to add the flavour of lime with out adding the lime.

- Mango Sour -

  • ABV - 4.0%
  • STYLE - Gose
  • IBU - 7
  • DRY HOPS - n/a

Clean, Sharp and Fruity. A refreshing 48 hour kettle soured Gose to sip in the sun, sharp but not too face puckering. Bonus points if you can guess how many mangos went into it?

- Ol' Red -

  • ABV - 5.2%
  • STYLE - Red IPA
  • IBU - 37
  • DRY HOPS - 4 g/l

Our take on a darker IPA which is based on Sierra Nevada's 'Torpedo', more malty than hoppy but still bitter enough to balance it out. Brewed in responce to "can you brew me a Doom Bar"
