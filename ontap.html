  • STYLE - Pale Ale

  • ABV - 4.0%

Clean, Sharp and Fruity. A refreshing kettle soured Gose to sip in the sun, guess how many mangos went into it?

- Ol' Red -

  • STYLE - Red IPA
  • IBU - 37
  • DRY HOPS - 4 g/l

Our take on a darker IPA based on Sierra Nevadas 'Torpedo', more malty than hoppy but still bitter enough to balance it out.
