~ Wedding Tap List ~

So you are trying the free stuff, brave.

Here's some more info to ease those concerns you may have, you know like the fact it was brewed in a shed. You didn't know that? Oh, well there's a website, must be legit right?

- PAW -

  • ABV - 4.5%
  • STYLE - Pale Ale
  • IBU - 45
  • DRY HOPS - 6 g/l

Pale Ale, refreshing and easy drinking like a British Pale but with enough hops to confuse it with an American. We are urrently on v3 of this recipie and this batch was fermented with Verdant's IPA yeast so it is a little more hazy.

- Sun Dog Millionare -

  • ABV - 5.5%
  • STYLE - Mexican Lager
  • IBU - 12
  • DRY HOPS - n/a

Our first lager, based upon the infamous Corona, bittered with a unique variety of hops from New Zealand to add the flavour of lime with out adding the lime.

Finish any of the above and we might be able to sneak in another keg

- Ol' Red -

  • ABV - 5.2%
  • STYLE - Red IPA
  • IBU - 37
  • DRY HOPS - 4 g/l

A darker IPA which is based on Sierra Nevada's 'Torpedo', more malty than hoppy but still bitter enough to balance it out. Brewed in responce to "can you brew me a Doom Bar"

- Half Way Crook -

  • ABV - 5.6%
  • STYLE - Milk Stout
  • IBU - 12
  • DRY HOPS - n/a

This one is a treasure hunt, limited bottles only......

A sweet stout adapted from our Oreo Stout recipie except this time we gave it a caffine kick in the form of Cold Brew Coffee. 1 litre to be exact, does that mean you can you drink it for breakfast, sure why not?
